About Our Projects.

Aphelion is the story of two male co-pilots, marooned on a desert planet, who must face what they fear the most: their inner selves. APHELION is a scifi media project that aims to rediscover via DIY the ancient techniques of classic science fiction, such as matte paintings, model miniatures, real sets.
